About SmartIntern

SmartIntern is an intelligent internship and scholarship discovery platform that provides personalized, data-driven recommendations to help students find the right opportunities at the right time.

Who we are

SmartIntern was built to address the challenge students face in discovering relevant internships and scholarships across multiple platforms. We aggregate verified opportunities and intelligently match them with students based on skills, interests, and academic profiles.

What we believe in

Accessibility

Every student deserves equal access to career opportunities.

Transparency

Only verified, trustworthy, and up-to-date information.

Personalization

Opportunities should match individual goals and strengths.
